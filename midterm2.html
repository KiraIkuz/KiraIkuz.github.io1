Hi there! My name is Juliette Bell. I am a student with the Media Arts and Sciences degree, and I want to be a indie game developer in the future!

Some games that inspired me to become the game developer that I want to become: Digimon, Hades, and Persona.

I love the evolution in Digimon, and the many routes you can take.

With Hades, I really love to play roguelikes, and the aspect of there being relationships that you can create with other characters was a favorite part of mine.

Lastly, with Persona, I love everything. The gameplay, the relationships, but especially the UI, if we are talking about Persona 5. That game is my favorite.
